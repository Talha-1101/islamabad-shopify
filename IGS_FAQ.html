How do I place an order?+
You can place an order directly on our website by adding items to your cart and checking out. Alternatively, you can message us on WhatsApp at 0332-6362390 and our team will assist you step by step — this is especially helpful for custom or large orders.
Can I order for someone else?+
Absolutely! This is exactly what most of our customers do. Simply provide the recipient's name, delivery address, and contact number when placing your order. You can also add a personalized message card, and the recipient will have no idea of the price unless you choose to include it.
What's the minimum order value?+
There is no strict minimum order value. However, same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 4:00 PM (PKT). Some remote delivery areas may have minimum requirements — feel free to ask us on WhatsApp for your specific area.
What if the item I want is out of stock?+
Message us on WhatsApp and we'll let you know when it's back in stock or suggest the closest available alternative. Our gifting specialists can also help you curate something custom that matches your vision and budget perfectly.
How far in advance should I order?+
For same-day delivery, order before 4:00 PM (PKT). For scheduled future delivery, we recommend ordering at least 24–48 hours in advance. For large events, weddings, or bulk corporate orders, we recommend 5–7 days advance notice to ensure everything is perfectly prepared.
Can I cancel or modify my order?+
You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it, provided it hasn't entered the preparation stage. For urgent modifications, please contact us immediately on WhatsApp at 0332-6362390. Once an order is in preparation, changes may not be possible for perishable items.
Do you offer bulk or corporate orders?+
Yes! We offer corporate gifting solutions for businesses, events, product launches, and employee appreciation programs. We provide custom packaging with your logo, bulk discounts, and dedicated account management. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
Do you offer same-day delivery?+
Yes! Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 4:00 PM (PKT) in Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Lahore, Karachi, Peshawar, and many surrounding areas. For smaller cities and towns, next-day delivery may apply. Contact us on WhatsApp to confirm for your specific area.
Which cities do you deliver to?+
We deliver nationwide across Pakistan including Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Lahore, Karachi, Peshawar, Quetta, Faisalabad, Multan, Sialkot, Gujranwala, Hyderabad, Mirpur, Wah Cantt, Haripur, Gujar Khan, and 50+ more cities. Message us if you're unsure about your area.
How will I know when the gift is delivered?+
We send you a WhatsApp notification with a delivery confirmation photo as soon as the gift has been handed over. For special requests, we can also send a pre-delivery photo showing how the gift looks before it's given — just let us know when ordering.
What if the recipient isn't home during delivery?+
Our rider will contact the recipient via phone to arrange an alternative time or location. If the recipient cannot be reached, we will contact you (the sender) to find the best solution. For perishable items, we always try to deliver directly to the person.
What are the delivery charges?+
Delivery charges vary by location and order size. Islamabad and Rawalpindi orders within city limits typically have a flat delivery fee. For outstation deliveries or other cities, charges are calculated based on the destination. The exact delivery fee is shown at checkout.
Can I request a specific delivery time?+
Yes! During checkout, you can specify a preferred delivery time slot (morning, afternoon, or evening). While we do our best to accommodate time-specific requests, exact minute-by-minute scheduling is subject to rider availability and traffic conditions. WhatsApp us for urgent time-specific requests.
Can I customize my gift?+
Absolutely — customization is one of our specialties. You can customize flower arrangements by color, size, and flower type; add personalized message cards with your handwritten text; customize cakes with photos, names, and designs; and build entirely bespoke gift bundles from scratch. Use our Build a Bundle page or WhatsApp us directly.
Can I add a personalized message card?+
Yes! Every order includes the option to add a free handwritten message card. Simply enter your message during checkout, and our team will include it beautifully written on premium stationery inside the gift packaging — completely private from the recipient unless you choose otherwise.
Can I request a specific flower color or type?+
Yes. If you have a preferred color palette or specific flowers in mind (like white roses, pink tulips, or purple orchids), let us know in the order notes or via WhatsApp. Subject to availability, our floral designers will do their best to match your vision.
Can I put a photo on a cake?+
Yes! We offer premium photo cakes where your chosen image is printed on edible paper and placed on the cake. Simply send us the photo via WhatsApp along with your order details. For best results, use a clear, high-resolution image. Photo cakes typically require 24 hours lead time.
Can I build a completely custom gift package?+
Yes! Use our Build a Bundle page to select your ideal combination of flowers, cakes, teddy bears, balloons, chocolates, and more. Alternatively, describe your vision to our team on WhatsApp and we'll curate the perfect gift based on your budget and occasion.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept: JazzCash, EasyPaisa, Bank Transfer (all major Pakistani banks), Visa/Mastercard (credit & debit cards), and Western Union / Remittance for overseas customers. All payments are processed securely and you receive an instant confirmation receipt.
Is my payment information secure?+
Yes. All online card payments are processed through Shopify's PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway with SSL encryption. We do not store your card details. Bank transfers and JazzCash/EasyPaisa transactions are processed through official, secure banking channels.
Can I pay on delivery (COD)?+
Cash on delivery is available for select orders within Islamabad and Rawalpindi. COD eligibility depends on the order value and delivery area. Please note that for high-value or custom orders, prepayment is required. Contact us on WhatsApp to check if COD is available for your order.
Do you offer any discounts or promo codes?+
Yes! Subscribe to our newsletter to get 10% off your first order. We also run seasonal promotions during Eid, Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, and other occasions. Follow us on Instagram and Facebook to stay updated on the latest offers and exclusive deals.
What currency are your prices in?+
All prices on our website are listed in Pakistani Rupees (PKR / Rs.). For overseas customers paying in USD, GBP, AED, or CAD, the equivalent amount is calculated at the current exchange rate at the time of payment. We accept international transfers via Western Union or Remittance services.
What is your return policy?+
Due to the perishable and personalized nature of most of our products (fresh flowers, custom cakes, etc.), we generally do not accept returns. However, if your order arrives damaged, incorrect, or significantly different from what was shown, we will replace or refund it immediately. See our full Refund Policy for details.
What if my order arrives damaged?+
We take full responsibility for any damage that occurs during delivery. If your order arrives in unacceptable condition, take a photo and send it to us on WhatsApp within 2 hours of delivery. We will arrange an immediate replacement or process a full refund — whichever you prefer.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?+
Customer satisfaction is our highest priority. If you are not satisfied with your order for a legitimate reason, please contact us on WhatsApp within 24 hours of delivery with photos. Our team will assess the issue and offer a replacement, partial refund, or full refund depending on the circumstances.
How long does a refund take?+
Once a refund is approved, it is typically processed within 3–7 business days depending on your payment method. JazzCash and EasyPaisa refunds are usually faster. Bank transfers may take 3–5 business days. Card refunds depend on your issuing bank's processing time.
Can I order from abroad?+
Yes! Sending gifts to Pakistan from abroad is our specialty. Thousands of Pakistanis living in the USA, UK, Canada, UAE, Australia, and beyond trust us to deliver beautiful gifts to their loved ones back home. Simply place your order online or message us on WhatsApp — we handle everything from there.
How do overseas customers pay?+
Overseas customers can pay via: International bank transfer (Wire), Western Union or MoneyGram, Remitly, WorldRemit, or other international remittance services. We provide our receiving account details upon request. Prices are quoted in PKR and converted at current exchange rates.
Will the recipient know the price of the gift?+
No. We never include prices or invoices with the gift delivery. All financial information is sent only to the person who placed the order. The recipient receives only the gift and your personalized message card — ensuring the surprise and emotional impact are fully preserved.
Do you send a photo proof of delivery?+
Yes! We understand overseas customers cannot physically verify delivery, so we always send a WhatsApp photo or video showing the delivered gift — giving you complete peace of mind. We can also arrange a pre-delivery photo so you can see the final arrangement before it's handed over.
