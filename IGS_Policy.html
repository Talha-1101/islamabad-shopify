Refund & Return Policy
Due to the perishable and personalized nature of our products — including fresh flowers, custom cakes, and personalized gifts — all sales are generally considered final. However, we are committed to your satisfaction and will make it right if something goes wrong.
Eligibility for Refunds

We will issue a full replacement or refund in the following circumstances:

  • The order was delivered significantly damaged or in unacceptable condition
  • The wrong item was delivered (not matching your order)
  • The order was not delivered at all and we cannot arrange re-delivery
  • The product quality was materially different from what was advertised
How to Claim a Refund

To initiate a refund or replacement claim:

  • Contact us via WhatsApp at 0332-6362390 or email at info@islamabadgiftshop.pk within 24 hours of delivery
  • Provide your order number and photos clearly showing the issue
  • Our team will review your claim within 2–4 business hours
  • If approved, you will receive a replacement within 24 hours or a refund within 3–7 business days
Non-Refundable Items
  • Perishable goods (flowers, cakes) where the issue is not due to our fault
  • Custom or personalized items that were made correctly per your specifications
  • Orders where delivery was refused by the recipient
  • Orders where incorrect delivery information was provided by the customer
  • Orders cancelled after the 2-hour cancellation window
Refund Processing Times
  • JazzCash / EasyPaisa: 1–3 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3–5 business days
  • Credit / Debit Card: 5–10 business days (depends on your bank)
  • International transfers: 5–10 business days

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how IslamabadGiftShop.pk ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit our website or use our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with:

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure server infrastructure, and restricted access to personal data. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@islamabadgiftshop.pk.

Shipping & Delivery Policy
Delivery Areas

We deliver across Pakistan with dedicated hubs in:

  • Islamabad Hub: Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Wah Cantt, Haripur, Gujar Khan, Jhelum, Mirpur, Attock
  • Lahore Hub: Lahore, Gujranwala, Gujrat, Sialkot, Faisalabad, Multan & across Punjab
  • Peshawar Hub: Peshawar, Mardan, Nowshera, Swabi, Charsadda & across KPK
  • Karachi Hub: Karachi, Hyderabad, Sukkur & across Sindh
Same-Day Delivery
  • Available in Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Lahore, Karachi, and Peshawar
  • Orders must be placed before 4:00 PM (PKT) for same-day delivery
  • Same-day delivery is subject to product and rider availability
  • Delivery typically occurs between 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM on the same day
Scheduled Delivery

You can schedule delivery for a future date up to 30 days in advance. For time-specific delivery requests (e.g., 7:00 PM exactly), please contact us on WhatsApp as this may involve additional coordination.

Delivery Charges
  • Delivery charges vary by location and are calculated at checkout
  • Free delivery may be offered during promotional periods
  • Rush delivery (within 2 hours) may incur additional fees
  • Remote or outstation areas may have higher delivery fees
Delivery Confirmation

Upon successful delivery, we will send you a WhatsApp notification with photo confirmation. If the recipient is unavailable at the time of delivery, our rider will contact both the recipient and sender to arrange an alternative.

Failed Deliveries

If a delivery fails due to:

  • Incorrect address provided by the customer — re-delivery charges apply
  • Recipient unavailable and unreachable — we will hold the order for 2 hours then contact you
  • Delivery refused by recipient — no refund is applicable
  • Our fault (e.g., rider error) — we will redeliver free of charge or issue a full refund
International Orders

We accept orders from customers worldwide. All gifts are delivered within Pakistan only — we do not ship internationally. For overseas customers, payment can be made via bank transfer, Western Union, or other international remittance services. Contact us for overseas customer support.
